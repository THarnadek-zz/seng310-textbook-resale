Textbook Resale Application

A project for SENG 310 Spring 2017

1. Creating a New Listing

1.1. Seller logs into app/site.

1.2. Seller presses the “Add Listing” button

1.3. Seller fills in information about what book they want to sell

  • Course name
  • Author
  • Book name

1.4. Seller adds tags for buyers to find their listing

1.5. Seller presses “Submit”, and the listing is posted on the site

2. Browsing Listings

2.1. Buyer opens the app/site

2.2. Buyer scrolls down the page

2.3. Buyer sees the new, or top listings offered by Sellers.

3. Advanced Searching

3.1. Buyer opens the app/site

3.2. Buyer enters in several search terms.

  • Price range
  • Newest to Oldest
  • Oldest to Newest
  • Seller ratings
  • Tags
  • Listing status

3.3. Buyer sees the listings that match their search requirements

4. Contacting Seller

4.1. Buyer logs into the app/site

4.2. Buyer goes to a listing that interests them

4.3. Buyer types in a comment on the listing, or uses the PM (Private Message) button on the listing

4.4. The Buyer waits for the Seller to receive their message

5. Updating Listing Status by Buyer

5.1. Seller logs in to the app/site

5.2. The listing was tagged as “Wanting to buy” by the Buyer.

5.3. The Seller can choose to change the status of the listing to “Pending”.

5.4. Once the listing has been sold, the Seller can manually enter the status of the listing as “Sold”

6. Updating Listing Status by App/Site

6.1. Buyer adds a “Wanting to buy” tag on their listing comment

6.2. The app/site automatically sets the listing status to “Pending”

6.3. Buyer and Seller are notified of the change in the listing status.  

6.4. Buyer can manually change the status to “Available” if they don’t want the status changed

7. Viewing Listing Status

7.1. Buyer opens the app/site

7.2. Buyer browses or searches any listing

7.3. Buyer looks at the status bar near the listing



8. Posting Bulk Listings

 

8.1. Seller logs in to the app/site

8.2. The Seller presses the
“Add” listing button

8.3. The Seller fills in the information regarding the books and tags

8.4. The Seller presses the “Bulk Listing” setting, then enters in the number of books they have

8.5. The Seller presses submit, and the bulk listing is posted on the app/site



9. Deleting a Listing

 

9.1. Seller logs into app/site

9.2. The Seller browses through their listings

9.3. The Seller removes a listing by clicking “Remove”

9.4. The listing is no longer available








10. Rating Experience with a  Seller

 

10.1. Buyer logs into app/site

10.2. Buyer goes to listings where Buyer and Seller have communicated.

10.3. Rating feature is enabled for those listings. Buyer can rate their experience with the Seller by picking out of 5 stars.


Buyer can also leave a comment.  

9.4. The rating results are updated on the Seller’s profile.
